About me

Welcome to my website! I'm Mahyar, a computer vision researcher at the University of Tabriz, where I dive deep into the fascinating world of medical imaging. Over the past four years, I've had the privilege of collaborating with brilliant minds like Dr. M. Zolfy, Dr. R. Rawwasizadeh, Dr. A. Ghavifekr, Dr. M. Rezai, and Dr. Z. Yasamani.
